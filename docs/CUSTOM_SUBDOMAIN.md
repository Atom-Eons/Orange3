# Custom Subdomain - not in v1.3

Deferred from v1.3. The "free custom subdomain" feature was scoped out before launch due to scaling cost uncertainty around managed tunnel pricing.

If you want remote access to AE See-Suite from another device, the current options are:

- LAN access: open the AE See-Suite machine's local IP at port 8787 from another device on the same network.
- Tailscale: free for personal use up to 100 devices, open-source, easy to set up, and works as a private VPN between your devices.

A polished AE See-Suite subdomain flow remains deferred until managed tunnel cost and privacy behavior are proven.
